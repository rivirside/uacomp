# Medical Student Government (MSG) — UA College of Medicine Phoenix
Source: https://www.uacomps.org/stugo

## Overview
Medical Student Government is the collective voice of students to UACOM-PHX. MSG is committed to the betterment of campus and works with the COM-PHX community to further enrich campus life and provide greater educational opportunities to colleagues.

Student Affairs at the Phoenix Campus houses student government. The Associate Dean of Student Affairs and Vice Dean of Undergraduate Medical Education advise the executive board.
