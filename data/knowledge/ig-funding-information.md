## Funding FAQ (Updated December 2026)

### Requirements for Event Funding Approval (per MSG constitution)
- Event must serve an **educational purpose**
- Event must be **open to all** students, staff, and faculty at UA COM-P
- Funds apply exclusively to UA-affiliated individuals
- **No alcoholic beverages** or illegal substances at the event
- Cannot involve religious activities, political activities, philanthropic donations, or unrelated commercial activities
- Must align with the UA College of Medicine – Phoenix mission statement

### Step-by-Step Event Planning Process

**Prior to Club Registration:**
1. Contact prospective speakers well in advance
2. Confirm date/time with speakers
3. Submit info via Club Registration Form

**Prior to the Event (after speaker confirmation):**
1. Add event details to club registration form
2. Send Room Request Form ASAP via the Room Request Smartsheet
3. Submit Funding Request Form a **minimum of 14 days in advance** of your event
   - Parking vouchers available at $16 each for guest speakers
   - Include tax, delivery, and tip (max 20%) in estimated total
   - Food restricted to lecture halls, classrooms, and student areas
4. After room confirmation, complete IG Events Form (club name, event title, date, time, room confirmation, description) and send to Communications Officer: webmaster@uacomps.org
5. Coordinate food order with approved vendors:
   - Email Baylee Duffy at bayleeduffy@arizona.edu with order details, date, time, location
   - Minimum **3 business days** notice required
   - Only one vendor per event without explicit Treasurer permission
   - Vendor must provide delivery, or an IG member must pick up
   - $35 minimum for grocery orders or fees apply
   - Order adjustments require 24 hours notice
6. Arrange food pickup or delivery on event day

**During the Event:**
- Photograph the event for the website
- Document what succeeded and challenges encountered
- Clean up afterward

**After the Event:**
- Provide Baylee with an itemized receipt (including tip amount) within 1–2 days
- Submit attendee list and event agenda
- Send thank-you to guest speakers
- Update IG Google Drive with event documentation for future leaders

### Finance Subcommittee Approval Guidelines
- All school requirements must be met
- Preference given to unique, hands-on collaborative events between multiple groups
- Lunch talk: **$15 per person, capped at $150 maximum**
- Simulation events: **capped at $150** (additional funds for non-standard Sim Center resources)
- **Maximum 1 lunch talk and/or 1 simulation event** approved per group (in addition to pre-semester events)

### Maximum Events Per Club
No set limit; the goal is a diverse schedule with lots of collaboration between groups.

### Speaker Travel Reimbursement
Case-by-case evaluation; may require Dean Briney or administrative approval to qualify as a school-sanctioned event.

### Personal Card Reimbursement Policy
Case-by-case; reimbursements are minimized and approved vendors are strongly preferred. Processed by the University finance department with possible denial. Timeline typically exceeds two months; deposits go to student Bursar account. Funding requests must still be submitted 14 days before events.

### Event Changes or Cancellations
Contact the treasurer immediately so unused funds can be reallocated.
