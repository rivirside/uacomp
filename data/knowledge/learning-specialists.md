## Overview
Student Development is designed to help students meet and exceed the challenges of being a successful medical student and lifelong learner.

Students have opportunities to:
- Engage in **consultations** related to learning and study strategies, STEP preparation, and life/school balance
- Enhance learning experiences through **wellness programming**, workshop opportunities, and tutoring
- Enrich academics through developing help-seeking behaviors, test-taking strategies, and self-management for high-stakes exams

## Services Available
- **Learning strategy consultations**: one-on-one meetings to build personalized study plans
- **STEP exam preparation support**: strategies for USMLE Step 1 and Step 2 CK
- **Wellness programming**: workshops and resources for stress management, burnout prevention, and work-life balance
- **Peer tutoring**: connect with trained medical student tutors in preclinical and clinical subjects
- **Test-taking workshops**: evidence-based techniques for multiple choice exam performance
- **Academic coaching**: help-seeking behaviors and self-regulation skills
