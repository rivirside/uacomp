# UAComp Student Interest Groups & Organizations

Last updated: 2026-02

## Medical Specialty Interest Groups

### Aerospace Medicine Interest Group
**URL:** https://www.uacomps.org/orgs/aerospace-medicine-interest-group
Dedicated to advancing knowledge in aerospace, hyperbaric, and dive medicine by exploring the unique physiological challenges of human health and performance in extreme environments. Through education, research, and collaboration with experts, the group prepares future physicians to innovate and improve safety in aviation, spaceflight, and underwater exploration.

### American Medical Association (AMA)
**URL:** https://www.uacomps.org/orgs/ama
The AMA's mission is to promote the art and science of medicine and the betterment of public health. At UACOM-Phoenix, the chapter focuses on advocacy, leadership, and education regarding health policy and physician activism while empowering students to shape medicine's future through informed engagement.

### American Medical Women's Association (AMWA)
**URL:** https://www.uacomps.org/orgs/amwa
Mission is to advance women in medicine and improve women's health through leadership, advocacy, education, and mentoring at local, national, and international levels. Their student chapter aims to empower women in medicine for healthcare leadership roles while encouraging broad participation in advocating for gender equity in the medical field.

### American Physician Scientists Association (APSA / PSIG)
**URL:** https://www.uacomps.org/orgs/psig
Aims to expose medical students to various types of research in clinical practice and increase interest in incorporating research into future medical careers. APSA hosts speakers from physician-scientists across multiple research domains and facilitates a journal club for students to discuss clinically relevant research.

### Anesthesiology Interest Group
**URL:** https://www.uacomps.org/orgs/anesthesiology
Aims to explore the multifaceted world of anesthesia through education, hands-on learning, research, and advocacy. The organization provides simulation training opportunities, faculty mentorship, and connects students with research in anesthesiology and perioperative medicine.

### Association of Women Surgeons (AWS)
**URL:** https://www.uacomps.org/orgs/association-of-women-surgeons
Mission is to empower women to pursue careers in surgical specialties by providing mentorship, shadowing opportunities, and presentations from female surgeons. AWS aims to increase female representation in surgery through connecting students with accomplished mentors and offering varied clinical experiences throughout the year.

### Cardiovascular Interest Group (CVIG)
**URL:** https://www.uacomps.org/orgs/cvig
Aims to provide an inclusive, engaging environment for anyone interested in learning more about the field of cardiology. They deliver meaningful insights into cardiovascular medicine through academic workshops, clinician speaker panels, and related events.

### Critical Care Interest Group (CCIG)
**URL:** https://www.uacomps.org/orgs/critical-care-interest-group-ccig
Aims to introduce medical students to critical care medicine through hands-on training in skills like airway and cardiac arrhythmia management. The organization offers educational opportunities including speaker sessions with professionals, demonstrations of critical care equipment, and mentoring to help students explore this specialty field.

### Dermatology Interest Group (DIG)
**URL:** https://www.uacomps.org/orgs/dig
Aims to educate medical students about dermatology through information, resources, and experiences. The organization hosts presentations from local dermatologists, organizes shadowing and volunteer opportunities, and conducts clinical skills sessions focused on dermatology procedures and advanced suturing techniques.

### Diagnostic Radiology Interest Group (DRIG)
**URL:** https://www.uacomps.org/orgs/radiology
Mission is to engage in the future of medicine by integrating patient care, innovation, and medical education through Diagnostic Radiology. The group focuses on expanding access to radiology education and fostering early exposure to image-guided procedures while promoting minimally invasive, patient-centered care.

### Emergency Medicine Interest Group (EMIG)
**URL:** https://www.uacomps.org/orgs/emig
Dedicated to fostering understanding of emergency healthcare through hands-on skills events, unique education opportunities, and collaboration with active EM professionals. Their primary goal is generating interest in emergency medicine by showcasing clinical skills, real-world scenarios, and mentorship programs for aspiring students.

### Environmental/Occupational Medicine Interest Group (EOMIG)
**URL:** https://www.uacomps.org/orgs/environmentaloccupational-medicine-interest-group-eomig
Dedicated to exploring the unique environmental and occupational health challenges that affect communities in Arizona, including extreme heat, water quality issues, and regional industries. Through mentorship and research opportunities, EOMIG encourages students to integrate environmental health into medical practice.

### Family Medicine Interest Group (FMIG)
**URL:** https://www.uacomps.org/orgs/fmig
Aims to serve the community, inspire students to pursue family medicine, and prepare leaders for the future of healthcare. The group provides hands-on clinical training in procedural skills while fostering connections with practicing physicians and residency programs.

### Gastroenterology & Hepatology Interest Group (GHIG)
**URL:** https://www.uacomps.org/orgs/gastroenterology-hepatology-interest-group
Mission is to foster a community of aspiring healthcare professionals dedicated to advancing knowledge, awareness, and collaboration in gastroenterology and hepatology. GHIG provides students with educational opportunities through lunch talks, workshops, mentorship, and shadowing experiences.

### Hematology-Oncology Interest Group (HOIG)
**URL:** https://www.uacomps.org/orgs/hematology-oncology-interest-group
Mission is to help medical students explore hematology and oncology through educational events, shadowing experiences, and research opportunities. HOIG aims to present both clinical and research perspectives in these rapidly evolving medical fields.

### Infectious Disease Interest Group (IDIG)
**URL:** https://www.uacomps.org/orgs/infectious-disease-ig
Aims to promote infectious disease as an exciting medical career choice while exposing students to the diversity of opportunities in the field. Demonstrates how ID impacts all aspects of medicine, healthcare, antimicrobial stewardship, public health, and global health through guest speakers, panels, and shadowing.

### Integrative and Preventive Medicine IG (IPMIG / IMIS)
**URL:** https://www.uacomps.org/orgs/imis
Aims to enhance and expand medical students' understanding of evidence-based complementary and alternative medicine. IPMIG provides educational opportunities in integrative, preventive, and lifestyle medicine while emphasizing physician wellness and community health.

### Internal Medicine Interest Group (IMIG)
**URL:** https://www.uacomps.org/orgs/imig
Aims to support COM-P medical students exploring internal medicine and its subspecialties while engaging local physicians. The organization hosts educational events and facilitates volunteer, shadowing, and research opportunities within the field.

### Interventional Radiology Interest Group (IRIG)
**URL:** https://www.uacomps.org/orgs/interventional-radiology-interest-group-irig
Aims to engage in the future of medicine by integrating patient care, innovation, and medical education through Interventional Radiology. They focus on expanding access to IR education, providing hands-on procedural training, and promoting minimally invasive, patient-centered care.

### Med-Peds Interest Group
**URL:** https://www.uacomps.org/orgs/med-peds-ig
Aims to introduce medical students to the combined residency training program in Internal Medicine and Pediatrics. The organization increases awareness through speaker events, mentorship opportunities, and conference attendance, supporting students interested in caring for patients across all ages.

### Neurosurgery Interest Group (NSurg)
**URL:** https://www.uacomps.org/orgs/neurosurgery-interest-group-nsurg
Mission is to foster a culture of collaboration, inclusivity, and productivity among learners and mentors engaging in neurosurgical research. Provides translational research opportunities by connecting learners with mentors at different training levels to develop neurosurgical skills and knowledge.

### Obstetrics & Gynecology IG (OB/GYN IG)
**URL:** https://www.uacomps.org/orgs/obgyn
Seeks to expose students to the field of obstetrics and gynecology while showcasing diverse career paths within the specialty. The organization provides practical learning opportunities and mentorship connections with physicians, while emphasizing patient perspectives and women's health education.

### Ophthalmology Interest Group (SIGO)
**URL:** https://www.uacomps.org/orgs/sigo
Aims to provide all students interested in ophthalmology with the resources and information needed to succeed in residency matching. Creates opportunities for clinical exposure, community outreach, and educational events while welcoming all medical students interested in eye health.

### Orthopaedic Surgery Interest Group (OSIG)
**URL:** https://www.uacomps.org/orgs/osig
Empowers medical students to explore orthopedic surgery careers through education, hands-on experiences, and mentorship. Provides seminars, shadowing, casting clinics, suture training, research opportunities, and social activities.

### Otolaryngology Interest Group (ENT IG)
**URL:** https://www.uacomps.org/orgs/otolaryngology-interest-group
Aims to provide the UACOM-P community with a look inside the diverse and innovative field of otolaryngology through interactive and educational events. Since ENT is typically absent from standard medical curricula, the organization fills this gap with simulation events and seminars from visiting specialists.

### Pathology Interest Group
**URL:** https://www.uacomps.org/orgs/pathology-interest-group
Aims to educate interested medical students about the scope and practice of pathology while fostering connections with local pathologists. Helps members explore the specialty and prepare for competitive pathology residency applications.

### Pediatrics Interest Group (PIG)
**URL:** https://www.uacomps.org/orgs/pig-1
Aims to broaden student exposure to pediatric medicine while supporting exploration of pediatric subspecialties and connecting students with medical professionals. PIG organizes hands-on events throughout the year and fosters networking opportunities with healthcare professionals.

### Perioperative and Pain Medicine IG
**URL:** https://www.uacomps.org/orgs/perioperative-and-pain-medicine-ig
Mission is to foster interest in pain medicine by providing education, mentorship, and clinical exposure while promoting evidence-based, multidisciplinary approaches to pain management. Supports medical students pursuing procedural medicine, research, and advocacy in pain management.

### Physical Medicine & Rehabilitation Interest Group (PM&R IG)
**URL:** https://www.uacomps.org/orgs/physical-medicine-rehabilitation-interest-group
Dedicated to providing education and exposure regarding Physical Medicine & Rehabilitation. Facilitates seminars, workshops, and clinical experiences to help students explore PM&R and understand how physiatrists treat conditions affecting the nervous and musculoskeletal systems while emphasizing functional recovery.

### Plastic Surgery Interest Group
**URL:** https://www.uacomps.org/orgs/plastic-surgery-interest-group
Aims to provide students with opportunities to explore the clinical, research, and procedural aspects of plastic surgery while fostering mentorship and professional development. Hosts graft demonstrations, suture clinics, and guest speaker sessions in an inclusive community.

### Psychiatry Interest Group (Psych IG)
**URL:** https://www.uacomps.org/orgs/pig
Aims to bridge the gap between medical education and psychiatric practice while cultivating a community committed to mental health mastery. Prioritizes clinical exposure, research, and mentorship to reduce stigma surrounding psychiatric conditions, while emphasizing physician wellness and health equity.

### Rural Medicine Interest Group (RMIG)
**URL:** https://www.uacomps.org/orgs/rural-medicine-interest-group-rmig
Aims to address the physician shortage in rural America, where approximately 20% of the U.S. population resides but only 11% of physicians practice. Provides students with opportunities to explore rural healthcare disparities, consider rural rotations or careers, and connect with rural physicians and communities.

### Ruth Jackson Orthopedic Society / Women in Orthopedics (RJOS)
**URL:** https://www.uacomps.org/orgs/ruth-jackson-orthopedic-society-rjoswomen-in-orthopedics
Aims to promote gender diversity within orthopedic surgery while supporting women's professional growth in the field. Facilitates mentorship connections, exposure to orthopedic careers, and networking through events like lunch talks with female surgeons and hands-on workshops.

### Sports Medicine Interest Group (SMIG)
**URL:** https://www.uacomps.org/orgs/sportsmed
Educates medical students about sports medicine in both primary care and orthopedic settings. Provides hands-on experience through community events such as free sports physicals for youth athletes, shadowing physicians at high school football games, and assisting with coverage at local races and triathlons.

### Student Interest Group in Neurology (SIGN)
**URL:** https://www.uacomps.org/orgs/sign
Aims to inspire medical students about neurology by showcasing amazing opportunities in the field and connecting students with networking, research, and educational resources. Highlights how neurology offers both acute hospital care and long-term chronic disease management with cutting-edge treatments.

### Surgery Interest Group (SIG)
**URL:** https://www.uacomps.org/orgs/surg
Aims to promote student exposure to and participation in general surgery and surgical subspecialties through educational events and networking. Organizes journal clubs, mentorship sessions, and talks featuring surgical professionals to help students explore various surgical career pathways.

### Urology Interest Group
**URL:** https://www.uacomps.org/orgs/urology-interest-group
Aims to foster a dynamic and inclusive community dedicated to advancing knowledge and interest in urology. Through educational events and networking with professionals, the organization helps students explore urology as a specialty and build connections with practicing urologists.

### Wilderness Medicine Interest Group (WMIG)
**URL:** https://www.uacomps.org/orgs/wilderness-medicine-interest-group-wmig
Seeks to prepare students for recognizing and managing injuries in outdoor settings while building competence in remote environments. Focuses on critical thinking, ingenuity, and adaptability through workshops, outdoor excursions, and skill-building that combine medical training with wilderness expertise.

## Non-Specialty Interest Groups

### Adverse Childhood Experiences / Trauma-Informed Care IG
**URL:** https://www.uacomps.org/orgs/adverse-childhood-experiencestrauma-informed-care-ig
Mission is to build an understanding of developmental trauma, Adverse Childhood Experiences (ACEs), and Trauma-Informed Care (TIC) among students, faculty, and staff at UA COM-P. Helps healthcare providers recognize how developmental trauma impacts patients and implement trauma-informed approaches to better serve those affected.

### Artificial Intelligence Interest Group (AI IG)
**URL:** https://www.uacomps.org/orgs/artificial-intelligence-ai-interest-group
Aims to explore how artificial intelligence may become integrated into healthcare as well as the ethics and legal challenges surrounding this area. Hosts educational talks with AI researchers and physicians, along with workshops to help students understand AI's real-world applications and implications in medicine.

### Asian Pacific American Medical Student Association (APAMSA)
**URL:** https://www.uacomps.org/orgs/asian-pacific-american-medical-student-association-apamsa
Dedicated to addressing the unique health challenges of Asian American, Native Hawaiian, and Pacific Islander (AANHPI) communities. Focuses on increasing awareness of health disparities affecting these populations through advocacy and outreach while fostering professional networking with community partners and the national APAMSA organization.

### Building the Next Generation of Academic Physicians
**URL:** https://www.uacomps.org/orgs/building-the-next-generation-of-academic-physicians
Helps diverse medical students and residents discover academic medicine as a career path and provides resources to further explore and embark on an academic medicine career. Supports students combining clinical practice with research through workshops, mentorship, and skill-building opportunities.

### Business in Medicine Interest Group
**URL:** https://www.uacomps.org/orgs/business-in-medicine
Mission is to increase understanding among future physicians of the intricacies and intersection of business, finance, and medicine. Empowers students to make informed decisions about finances, investments, and how economic forces shape healthcare delivery and patient outcomes.

### Catholic Medical Association (CMA)
**URL:** https://www.uacomps.org/orgs/catholic-medical-association-cma-1
Aims to form and support medical students in living and promoting Catholic Faith principles within medical science and practice. Provides fellowship and community through masses, meals, and talks from local Catholic physicians, welcoming all students interested in this faith-based medical community.

### Chess Club
**URL:** https://www.uacomps.org/orgs/chess-club
A student-led organization dedicated to providing a casual, welcoming environment where everyone of all experience levels can learn and enjoy chess together. Combines intellectual development with social connection while leveraging chess's cognitive benefits and fostering camaraderie among medical students.

### Christian Medical Society (CMS)
**URL:** https://www.uacomps.org/orgs/cms
Aims to create community for Christian healthcare students through interaction, encouragement, support, and service. Serves as a fellowship group fostering personal, professional, and spiritual growth while connecting members with mentors and service opportunities.

### Climbing Cats
**URL:** https://www.uacomps.org/orgs/climbing-cats
Dedicated to fostering the physical and mental well-being of students through engaging climbing events. Organizes regular climbing activities to provide medical students with stress relief and a healthy outlet while building community connections regardless of experience level.

### Country Dance Cats
**URL:** https://www.uacomps.org/orgs/country-dance-cats
Aims to provide an opportunity for beginners and pros to experience Country dancing around the Phoenix area. Teaches foundational skills in Country Swing and Line dancing while organizing outings to local dance venues.

### Culinary Medicine Interest Group
**URL:** https://www.uacomps.org/orgs/culinary-medicine-interest-group
Seeks to enhance understanding and practical use of healthy, nutritious foods among future medical professionals, patients, and community members. By combining culinary arts education with hands-on experiences, the group helps future physicians understand food as a preventive health tool and guide patients toward healthier lifestyles.

### Etymology Interest Group / Leech
**URL:** https://www.uacomps.org/orgs/etymology-ig-leech
Mission is to explore the linguistic roots and stories behind medical terminology, helping students remember concepts more easily. Functions as a journal club that identifies historical and etymologic connections in medical terms through monthly meetings discussing key course material.

### Fitness Interest Group
**URL:** https://www.uacomps.org/orgs/fitness-ig
Mission is to support medical student well-being by encouraging regular physical activity, balanced fitness habits, and a culture of wellness throughout medical training. Promotes physical activity and overall wellness in an inclusive environment for students across all fitness levels.

### Futbol Doctors
**URL:** https://www.uacomps.org/orgs/futbol-doctors
Dedicated to building community and balance among medical students through a shared love of soccer. Organizes World Cup watch parties, FIFA tournaments, and casual scrimmages, focusing on promoting well-being, friendship, and joy while supporting excellence in medical training.

### Harm Reduction & Addiction Medicine
**URL:** https://www.uacomps.org/orgs/harm-reduction-addiction-medicine
A dual CHIP program and Interest Group committed to improving medical student education in addiction medicine and supporting people who use drugs (PWUD) in the community. Focuses on reducing negative consequences of drug use through practical strategies while challenging stigma and advocating for patient-centered policies on substance use.

### IGNITEMED
**URL:** https://www.uacomps.org/orgs/ignitemed
A program curated for the current medical student with interactive lectures, brief high-yield topic discussions, and group coaching from national experts on topics typically absent from standard medical education. Founded by women physicians specifically for female medical students, addressing the distinctive obstacles women face in medicine.

### Jazz Interest Group
**URL:** https://www.uacomps.org/orgs/jazz-interest-group
Aims to bring medical students and fellow jazz enthusiasts together to foster deeper appreciation of live jazz performances in the Phoenix area. Promotes jazz music's cultural significance and historical legacy through monthly performances and collaborative events.

### Jewish Medical Student Association (JMSA)
**URL:** https://www.uacomps.org/orgs/jmsa
Aims to promote a venue for social, cultural, and educational opportunities relating to Judaism and the medical field. Creates a welcoming community for Jewish medical students to celebrate their heritage, engage with Jewish healthcare values, and participate in holiday celebrations and professional networking.

### Latino Medical Student Association (LatinoMed / LMSA)
**URL:** https://www.uacomps.org/orgs/latinomed
Mission is to unite and empower current and future physicians through service, mentorship, and education to advocate for the improved health of the Hispanic and Latina/o/x community. Aims to unify Latino medical students, promote recruitment and retention, educate members on Latino health disparities, and create leadership opportunities.

### Law and Medicine Interest Group
**URL:** https://www.uacomps.org/orgs/law-and-medicine-interest-group
Aims to educate students about the intricacies of medical practice and health law by hosting seminars and discussions with experts. Helps future physicians understand healthcare law complexities and explore career opportunities at the intersection of medicine and law.

### LGBTQIA+ In Medicine
**URL:** https://www.uacomps.org/orgs/lgbtq-in-medicine
Mission is to foster an inclusive, supportive community for LGBTQ+ medical trainees and allies, promote health equity for LGBTQ+ patients, and advocate for improved understanding of queer health through education, mentorship, and service.

### Medical Ethics Interest Group (MEIG)
**URL:** https://www.uacomps.org/orgs/medical-ethics-interest-group
Aims to inform and educate the UACOM-P community on prominent issues in medical ethics while equipping students to navigate ethical challenges in clinical practice. Facilitates learning through case discussions and expert talks, while promoting advocacy for equitable healthcare and careers in medical ethics.

### Medical French Interest Group
**URL:** https://www.uacomps.org/orgs/medical-french-interest-group
Mission is to promote proficiency in medical French while fostering cultural competence, global health awareness, and service to French-speaking patient populations through education, collaboration, and community engagement. Helps future physicians communicate effectively with French-speaking patients and engage in global health settings.

### Medical Students for Choice (MS4C)
**URL:** https://www.uacomps.org/orgs/ms4c
Dedicated to ensuring comprehensive reproductive healthcare access and training. Works to integrate abortion education and training into standard medical curricula while supporting student advocacy in reproductive health.

### Meditation Interest Group
**URL:** https://www.uacomps.org/orgs/meditation-interest-group
A student-led group that promotes mental health and wellness through meditation and mindfulness practices. Welcomes participants of all experience levels and provides a supportive environment for students to develop contemplative skills during medical school.

### Med Mentors
**URL:** https://www.uacomps.org/orgs/med-mentors
Aims to empower and guide pre-med students on their journey to medical school by providing mentorship, workshops, and resources. Focuses on supporting underrepresented and first-generation students through partnerships with UACOM-P's pipeline programs and longitudinal mentor-mentee relationships.

### Middle East and North Africa Medical Student IG (MENA IG)
**URL:** https://www.uacomps.org/orgs/middle-east-and-north-africa-mena-medical-student-interest-group
Mission is to integrate the rich traditions of Middle Eastern and North African cultures into the medical field through cultural events, mentorship, and volunteer opportunities. Focuses on building an inclusive community that connects students with peers and professionals across medical institutions while promoting culturally competent healthcare.

### Music in Medicine IG
**URL:** https://www.uacomps.org/orgs/music-in-medicine-ig
Enables students to pursue their musical interests while exploring healthcare applications through interactive workshops centered around the application of music in healthcare settings. Volunteers with patients, particularly those with dementia at facilities like Hospice of the Valley.

### Muslim Medical Students Association (MMSA)
**URL:** https://www.uacomps.org/orgs/mmsa
Mission is to empower Muslim medical students by offering opportunities for spiritual growth, professional development, and community building. Additionally, helps physicians understand the unique healthcare needs of Muslim patients while fostering culturally competent medical care.

### Native American Service & Equity (NASE)
**URL:** https://www.uacomps.org/orgs/native-american-service-and-equity-nase-ig
Aims to educate medical students about and provide opportunities to serve Native American populations. Focuses on addressing health disparities, promoting community outreach, and amplifying Native voices to advance health equity initiatives.

### Orthodox Christian Student Association
**URL:** https://www.uacomps.org/orgs/orthodox-christian-association
Aims to support Orthodox Christian students and those interested in Orthodox Christianity through worship, service, education, and fellowship. Creates space for spiritual growth and discussion while fostering respectful dialogue across different faith traditions within the medical school community.

### Point of Care Ultrasound IG (POCUS IG)
**URL:** https://www.uacomps.org/orgs/pocus-ig
Aims to foster a dynamic community passionate about advancing point-of-care ultrasound. Provides interactive workshops and hands-on learning experiences to equip medical students with the clinical knowledge and practical ultrasound skills needed for clerkships and future careers.

### RAD-AID
**URL:** https://www.uacomps.org/orgs/rad-aid
A student-run chapter dedicated to enhancing access to medical imaging in underserved regions globally. Connects members with physicians engaged in international radiology outreach and facilitates research opportunities and participation in the RAD-AID Medical Student Program and annual conference.

### Recreational Sports Interest Group
**URL:** https://www.uacomps.org/orgs/recreational-sports-interest-group
Mission is to promote physical health, mental well-being, and community among medical students through inclusive, low-pressure recreational sports opportunities. Facilitates regular games in volleyball, soccer, and basketball, welcoming participants regardless of skill level while emphasizing teamwork and stress relief.

### Ringside Med
**URL:** https://www.uacomps.org/orgs/ringside-med
Aims to provide relief for ringside physicians and help make combat sports safer by having medical students assist with MMA and boxing events in Arizona. Members shadow licensed inspectors before becoming licensed themselves, then help physicians monitor fighter vitals and neurological status to identify potential traumatic brain injuries.

### South Asian Medical Student Association (SAMSA)
**URL:** https://www.uacomps.org/orgs/south-asian-medical-student-association
Aims to unify South Asian medical students across the United States while raising awareness of healthcare issues affecting South Asian communities. Creates spaces for cultural celebration, professional networking, and educational discussions about health concerns relevant to this demographic.

### Spoke Squad
**URL:** https://www.uacomps.org/orgs/spoke-squad
A cycling club focused on connecting med students who want to stay active and explore Arizona's beautiful trails. Emphasizes community bonding, outdoor recreation, and wellness breaks from academic demands.

### Student National Medical Association (SNMA)
**URL:** https://www.uacomps.org/orgs/student-national-medical-association-snma
Dedicated to supporting and empowering underrepresented minority medical students while tackling healthcare disparities. Combines mentorship, community service, and professional development to build compassionate leaders who advance health equity in underserved communities.

### Students for a National Health Program
**URL:** https://www.uacomps.org/orgs/students-for-a-national-health-program
A political advocacy group dedicated to advancing universal healthcare, believing that healthcare is a human right with three essential components: quality, affordability, and accessibility. Engages with legislators, educates students about healthcare systems, and organizes campaigns to promote systemic change.

### SYNAPSE
**URL:** https://www.uacomps.org/orgs/synapse
Creates an inclusive space for UA COM-P students to explore movement and express themselves through choreography, welcoming participants regardless of experience level. Emphasizes performance dance styles like street and contemporary, offering workshops and classes while ensuring students of all physical abilities can participate.

### T1D Care Interest Group
**URL:** https://www.uacomps.org/orgs/t1d-care-interest-group
Aims to educate medical students about type 1 diabetes (T1D) and how to best provide continuing care for patients with T1D, while providing outreach opportunities to support and educate the Phoenix T1D community. Also creates campus community for medical students living with T1D while connecting with the broader Phoenix diabetes community.
